About the Project

Luxury skincare that respects its audience.

Veero is a skincare brand designed for women 50 and above. A demographic that most beauty brands either ignore or condescend to. The design brief was clear: no anti-aging language, no anxiety, no before-and-after logic.

Instead, Veero positions itself around ritual, earned confidence, and material quality. The visual language draws from high-end stationery, fine ceramics, and editorial fashion — not pharmaceutical packaging.
